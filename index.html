Welcome to

Trinidad and Tobago's leading Recruitment & HR Consultancy.

For 23 years, Regency Recruitment and Resources Limited has provided local and multinational firms with support professionals to fill a wide range of temporary, contract and permanent work. As the leading local “One Stop HR Shop” Regency provides a complete range of cost-effective and flexible Human Resource Management services, including:

Payroll Solutions

Regency offers convenient and affordable payroll solutions for contract employees. This can be offered to any personnel, whether recruited by Regency or not.


Expatriate Orientation

Expatriates and their families may have a hard time adjusting to a new country. We provide specialized assistance to help facilitate an easier transition.


Customized Training Programmes

We offer a range of unique customized training programmes. This includes Business Etiquette, Customer Service and much more.



Recruitment and Selection

We provide local and multinational firms across Trinidad and Tobago with support professionals to fill temporary, contract and permanent work.

Resume Counselling

Our one-on-one sessions with experienced recruitment consultants take individuals through all the ways they can improve their resume.


Transition Services

We have set up specialized employee transition services center where we offer a wide range of support to retrenched staff over a fixed period of time.
